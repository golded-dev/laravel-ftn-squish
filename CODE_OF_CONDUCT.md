# Code Of Conduct

Be direct. Be useful. Do not be a pain on purpose.

This project is small and technical. Good disagreement is welcome. Personal attacks, harassment, bigotry, and drive-by hostility are not.

## Expected Behavior

- Keep feedback about the work.
- Explain the concrete problem.
- Assume maintainers have finite time and ordinary human battery levels.
- Stay on topic.

## Not Accepted

- harassment or threats
- sexualized or discriminatory language
- personal insults
- publishing private information
- repeated off-topic arguing after being asked to stop

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block accounts, or otherwise end interactions that make the project worse.

No theatre. Just boundaries.
